Motivations

Since I am interested in Interdisciplinary study, and interactive art is about combining the use of technology with the expression of art, I would be really willing to explore deeper in this field and make creative works.

I believe interactive projects could lead to positive social change. The possible result of bringing light to the minority really motivates me a lot.

With more empathy to emerge among all human beings, negative and wrong stereotypes would reduce and even disappear. As along as people start to truly understand each other, the whole world would be better.
